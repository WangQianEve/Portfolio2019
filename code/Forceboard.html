Forceboard
Pressure-based Text Entry
Advisors: Prof. Yuanchun Shi, Prof. Chun Yu Collaborators: Mingyuan Zhong, Xuhai Xu
SIGCHI 2018 Paper Source Code
ForceTouch describes the idea of leveraging pressure to type. Users apply pressure on the interface to position a sliding cursor over the desired letter and confirm the selection with a quick release.

We used iphone's 3D touch technology to detect pressure, and used Google glass to show the interface in this demo.
